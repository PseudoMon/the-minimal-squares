The Thoughts Behind All This

21 June 2019 Filed Under: Design

The Minimal Squares is a minimalist design for a simple blog or blog-like site. It focus more on the words, replacing the typical big splash-image of most website with a large snippet of, in this case, the latest blog post. This part of the page can also contain an infobox, a large hello, or even a splash image, if you feel like it.

Click on the Read More to this post to see example of a single post. Click on Archive above to see example of a page that shows the list of posts.
